Pasta recipe

A very famous and delicious Italian Cuisine

Pasta is a very diverse and popular food across the world that can come in many forms

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta noodles in water for 10 mins
  2. Cut the tomatoes into diced slices
  3. Put the tomatoes and meatballs into a pan and simmer till cooked
  4. Put the pasta and tomatoes together on a plate
