Sushi recipe

A very yummy Japanese Cuisine

Sushi is a Japanese original cuisine that has been around for many centuries and is very flexible and can be used with many ingredients

Ingredients

  • Seaweed squares
  • Sushi rice
  • Prawn
  • Avacado

Steps

  1. Cut avacado and prawn into small 5cm rectangles
  2. Lay seaweed against the table
  3. Put rice on seaweed
  4. Put avacado and prawn on top of the rice
  5. Fold over the seaweed to form a cylinder shape for sushi
